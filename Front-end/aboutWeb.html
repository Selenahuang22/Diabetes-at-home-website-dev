About This Website

Diabetes@Home is a digital health solution software built by 5 students studying in the University of
Melbourne. This website aims to help patients manage their diabetes at home and help clinicians to
monitored the situations of their patients remotely. Patients can record health data, view data they
have recorded and add comments. Clinicians can track their patients by monitoring their health data,
encourage their patients by sending messages to them, and view patientsâ€™ comments. It is very
user-friendly and learnable. The patient UI works well on phone, tablet and desktop sized screens
while the clinician UI only works on desktop sized screens. Both clinicians and patients need to log
in in order to access most of the functions.
